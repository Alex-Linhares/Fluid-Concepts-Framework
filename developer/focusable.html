Focusable¶

Mixin class to be added to anything that can be the focus in the stream.

class farg.core.focusable_mixin.FocusableMixin[source]¶

A mixin for things that want to be part of the stream.

It is a pure interface — all methods die horribly when called unless over-ridden.

GetSimilarityAffordances(focusable, other_fringe, my_fringe, controller)[source]¶

Potential actions to take in response to a fringe overlap.

When the fringe of a stored focus overlaps the fringe of a newly focused entity, this function is called. Here, self is the older focus, and my_fringe is its fringe. focusable is the new focusable, and other_fringe is its fringe.

Args:
focusable: The most recent focus. other_fringe: Fringe of ‘focusable’. my_fringe: Fringe of ‘self’, which is the older focusable. controller: controller for this subspace (or whole app if this is the top space).
Returns:
A list of codelets that follow-up on this potential similarity.
