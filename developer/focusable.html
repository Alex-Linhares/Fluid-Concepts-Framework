GetSimilarityAffordances(focusable, other_fringe, my_fringe, controller)[source]¶

Potential actions to take in response to a fringe overlap.

When the fringe of a stored focus overlaps the fringe of a newly focused entity, this function is called. Here, self is the older focus, and my_fringe is its fringe. focusable is the new focusable, and other_fringe is its fringe.

Parameters:
  • focusable – The most recent focus.
  • other_fringe – Fringe of ‘focusable’.
  • my_fringe – Fringe of ‘self’, which is the older focusable.
  • controller – controller for this subspace (or whole app if this is the top space).
Returns:

A list of codelets that follow-up on this potential similarity.
